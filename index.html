RadAnnotate

RadAnnotate is an AI-powered platform designed to automate and accelerate the annotation of clinical radiology reports. Leveraging advanced language models, RadAnnotate identifies and labels anatomical entities and clinical observations, enabling seamless integration into medical workflows.

Built with a focus on accuracy, explainability, and user-friendly interaction, RadAnnotate bridges the gap between AI innovation and clinical applicability, ensuring high-quality annotations even in complex medical narratives.
